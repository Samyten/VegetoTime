Vegeto Time Chrome Extension Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2025

Vegeto Time is a browser extension that provides users with notifications for Crafting Vegeto’s Twitch livestreams and YouTube video uploads, along with a countdown timer to the next scheduled stream.

Information Collection and Use

Vegeto Time does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.
All extension settings, preferences, and alert history are stored locally within your browser and are never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Permissions

The extension requests only the permissions necessary to:

  • Display notifications in your browser.
  • Periodically check Twitch and YouTube APIs for new content.
  • Store your notification preferences and extension settings locally.
No personally identifiable information, browsing history, or sensitive data is accessed or collected.

Data Sharing

No data is shared, sold, or transferred to any third party under any circumstances.

Third-Party Services

Vegeto Time uses the official Twitch and YouTube APIs to check for new streams and videos. No user information is sent to or received from these services—only public data about Crafting Vegeto’s content is retrieved.

Changes to this Policy

If any changes are made to this privacy policy, users will be notified via an update to this page.
